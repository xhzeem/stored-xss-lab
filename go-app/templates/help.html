Help & Documentation

Everything you need to know about using the Support Desk.

Getting Started

The Support Desk is your one-stop portal for IT support requests. You can submit tickets, chat with support staff, and track the status of your requests.

Submitting a Ticket

Navigate to Tickets and click "Create New Ticket". Fill in the subject, description, and select a priority level. Your ticket will be queued for review by our support team.

Live Chat

For quick questions, use the Live Chat feature. Messages are visible to all team members, so avoid sharing sensitive information.

Notifications

Check the Notifications page for updates on your tickets, assignments, and system announcements.

Memos

The Memo Board contains internal communications from management and team leads. Important policy changes and announcements are posted here.

Release Notes

Track platform updates on the Releases page. New features, bug fixes, and maintenance schedules are documented here.

CSV Import

Use CSV Import to bulk-upload data such as employee records, inventory lists, or contact information. Enter data as comma-separated values.

Saved Searches

Save frequently used search queries on the Searches page for quick access. Use filters like status:open, priority:high, or subject:keyword.

Admin Panel

Administrators can access the Admin Queue to manage all tickets, assign priorities, and preview ticket details.
